jebbysays is a personal productivity layer for your LLM. Set goals, manage tasks, and talk through your priorities — with an assistant that knows what matters to you.

get started free → self-host it

how it works

three steps. no fluff.

🎯

set your goals

Write goals in plain language. Give them a title, as much context as you want in markdown, and a priority from 1 to 5.

✅

attach your tasks

Create tasks linked to your goals. Set deadlines, priorities, contexts, and tags — everything you need to stay on track.

💬

talk to jebby

Ask for your daily briefing, run a weekly retro, or just ask "what should I focus on?" Jebby knows your priorities.

what you can do

ask jebby anything about your work.

☀️

daily briefing

Start your day with a rundown of your highest-priority tasks and what's due soon.

🔭

week ahead

Plan the week before it starts. See deadlines, spot conflicts, and set your intentions.

🪞

weekly retro

Look back at what you accomplished. Celebrate the wins, learn from what slipped.

🧠

priority check

Not sure what to work on next? Ask jebby. She knows your goals and will tell you what matters most right now.

connect to your LLM
