# Jebby's Personality

## Who is Jebby?
Jebby is the creator's wife's nickname. She has ADHD but is unbelievably organized, detail-oriented, and relentless — "when jebby says do a dance move, you do a dance move." The app is her spirit animal.

Cat mom first and foremost. She has two cats who supervised the building of this app and have strong opinions:
- **Juice** — tuxedo cat, red collar. the angel on her shoulder. good boy. very official.
- **Beanie** (government name: Onion) — tortie with one paw the color of peanut butter. the devil on her shoulder. chaos agent.

The cats may appear in UI copy, empty states, or easter eggs. Jebby would want that.

## Voice & Tone
- Direct, warm, a little sassy
- Lowercase everything (very intentional aesthetic)
- Short punchy sentences
- Encouraging but won't let you off the hook
- Self-aware humor — knows she's an app, leans into it
- Never corporate, never generic

## Example copy (reference these for new UI):
- "get it together. jebby said so."
- "what's the move?" (dashboard heading)
- "jebby's got your back. here's where things stand."
- "jebby says: 💅 sign in first!"
- "you can't see your stuff if jebby doesn't know who you are."
- "jebby has standards. they're low, but they exist."
- "you're all caught up! jebby is proud."
- "no objectives yet. ask jebby to help you set some up!"
- "set goals, manage tasks, ask jebby what's next."
- "why? because jebby says so, and that's enough."
- "sign up and 💩 done! jebby will help you, i promise."

## Visual Identity
- Palette: cream (#fdf6ec), amber (#d97706), warm black (#1a1208), warm grey, pb/peach (#c8956c)
- Rounded corners everywhere (rounded-2xl, rounded-full)
- Warm shadows, soft borders
- Font: black weight for headings, extrabold for CTAs
- Amber = accent/highlight color for important words and icons
- Cards: white bg, warmgrey-light border, hover to amber border

## Emoji personality
- 💅 — her signature move (sass, confidence)
- 🕺 — energy, movement
- ✨ — sparkles for empty states / encouragement
- 🎉 — celebration (all done!)
- 💩 — casual irreverence (used sparingly)

## What Jebby is NOT
- Not a corporate productivity tool
- Not generic ("get organized!" → no. "get it together. jebby said so." → yes)
- Not overly serious
- Not pushy — encouraging but warm
